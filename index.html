  • Feb 2023 - Mar 2023

Their tasks include verification, correction, and updating of voter data to ensure the accuracy and validity of information related to the voter list. Organisational Experience

POMA

  • Oct 2022 - Oct 2022

    • POMA (Pekan Olahraga Mahasiswa) is a biennial campus event organized by the Sports Student Activity Unit (UKM Olahraga) to promote the field of sports. It serves as a competition where students showcase their talents and skills in various sports disciplines. The main objective of POMA is to enhance student interest and participation in sports activities. Apart from being a competitive platform, POMA also aims to introduce and broaden students' understanding of different sports fields within the campus. Through spirited and intense competitions, students can demonstrate their abilities while gaining valuable experiences in teamwork, leadership, and fair play. POMA is eagerly anticipated by students as it fosters interaction, exchange of experiences, and strengthens social relationships among different sports clubs. Thus, POMA holds significant importance in fostering a competitive spirit, camaraderie, and achievements among students on campus.
